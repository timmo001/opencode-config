# QA Storyboard Checklist

## Narrative Clarity

- The primary actor is obvious at every beat
- Sequence order matches interaction importance
- Motion direction supports spatial logic

## Timing and Rhythm

- Beat durations are consistent with product motion tokens
- Stagger offsets are intentional and not excessive
- Exit timing is not slower than enter for routine flows

## Usability

- Interaction acknowledgment appears within `80ms`
- Motion does not block completion of core tasks
- Rapid repeated interactions do not break choreography

## Accessibility

- Reduced-motion mode keeps sequence understandable
- Focus and keyboard navigation remain correct
- No essential information is conveyed by motion alone

## Performance

- No visible frame drops in common usage
- No unnecessary layout-heavy animation properties
- Layer count and long tasks remain controlled during sequences
